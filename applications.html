Who are we looking for?

We're seeking passionate individuals who are not just enthusiasts but visionaries, those who are eager to explore, learn, and contribute their unique perspectives. Whether you're a filmmaker, a tech wizard, a storyteller at heart, or someone passionate about the vibrant hues of Asian culture, there's a place for you at CME.

Join us in our endeavor to share the vibrant spectrum of Asian traditions, stories, and experiences. Embrace the opportunity to become part of a dynamic team dedicated to creating unforgettable moments that transcend boundaries and ignite a profound appreciation for the diversity of Asian cultures. Together, let's paint the future of media and entertainment with a kaleidoscope of cultural richness.
