About CME

Founded in 2013, Media & Entertainment at Cornell (CME) aims to integrate media and entertainment, capturing imaginations and invoking senses in our events. Our core mission is to promote Asian culture and to foster a great understanding through sharing Asian traditions, stories, and experiences.
