Events Hosted by CME

CME events blend media and entertainment seamlessly, capturing imaginations and invoking the senses. Whether it's the eerie allure of Halloween Party, the rhythmic beats of Cornell Rapping Competition, Heart Signal's romantic vibes, or the Food Station's culinary adventures, every gathering is an emblematic celebration of artistry and talent.

EVENTS

04/2023

Rap of Cornell

The rap competition provided a dynamic stage for participants to unleash their passion and showcased a golden opportunity to display their talents. Here, like-minded contestants united to share their love for hip-hop culture, fostering mutual exchange and learning. It was an environment where individuals not only demonstrated their unique personalities and styles but also explored the boundless possibilities within hip-hop music.

10/2023

Halloween Neon Dream

An elegant Halloween affair featuring professional face painting, a themed photo booth, and engaging game stations. Guests savored curated beverages, danced to refined beats spun by our DJ, and discovered sweet surprises at Trick or Treat stations. It was a night of sophisticated celebration, where everyone donned their best costumes and reveled in the enchanting atmosphere!

10/2023

Fundraising Station

Bingo Fundy led a joyous and challenging street performance game. Participants selected tasks ranging from changing WeChat profile pictures to recreating humorous videos, adding a lively dimension to the event. Additionally, authentic Chinese snacks such as cold pot skewers, jianbing, and roujiamo were featured, providing a delectable taste of Chinese cuisine.
